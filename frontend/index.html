Methodology & Formulas

Opportunity Score (0-100)

Calculates the urgency of digital transformation. Evaluates the website availability (+40 if missing), review volume (+5 to +15), rating severity (+10 to +20), and missing phone detail (+5). Weights are configured in config/scoring.json.

Data Quality Score (0-100)

Calculates dataset integrity. Starts at 100. Deducts for missing critical parameters (Website -20, Phone -15, Name/City/Address -10 each), duplicate records (-10), standardizations (-5), and missing optional properties (-2 each).

Upload Custom Dataset

Import your own Google Maps business listing. The system case-insensitively maps available fields and validates mandatory columns.
